Get to know me!

Hey there! I'm Eloura, a passionate UX designer with a love for creating fun and intuitive digital experiences. With a background in Customer Service and Management, and a knack for design, I bridge the gap between human behavior and innovative technology. When I'm not wireframing or prototyping, you can find me exploring nature trails for painting inspiration, experimenting with new cooking recipes, or getting lost in a good video game.

My journey in UX design has been an exciting adventure filled with learning, creativity, and a lot of coffee. I thrive on solving complex problems and turning ideas into user-friendly realities.

I am currently enrolled at Springboard for UX/UI Design and will be completing my course soon.

I have also spent time as a UX Intern for a startup company called FoodMagnet, working as a Research Intern and creating easy flows for my client and users to understand.
